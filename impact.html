Success Stories

Empowering Through Education

Through our NextGen IT Program, we've seen students gain confidence and digital skills that open doors to new opportunities. One graduate went on to secure an internship at a local tech company, becoming the first in their family to work in the technology sector.

"The IT program changed my life. I never thought I could work with computers, but now I have skills that will help me build a better future." - Program Participant

Growing Food Security

Our gardening project at a local school has transformed a barren piece of land into a thriving vegetable garden. Students now learn about nutrition, sustainability, and entrepreneurship while producing fresh produce for their families and school meals.

"The garden teaches our children so much more than just growing food. It teaches them responsibility, teamwork, and the value of hard work." - School Principal

Feeding the Future

Our Hearts of Provision Feeding Scheme has served thousands of meals to children in need. One mother shared how the program helped her family during a difficult period, ensuring her children never missed a meal despite financial challenges.

"Knowing my children have a nutritious meal at school gives me peace of mind. Thank you, Lefika Hearts." - Community Member

Women Leading Change

Through our Women & Youth Empowerment program, we've helped establish a local women's cooperative that now provides income opportunities for over 20 women in the community. The cooperative produces and sells handmade products, creating sustainable livelihoods.

"The empowerment program gave me the confidence and skills to start my own business. Now I can provide for my family." - Program Graduate
