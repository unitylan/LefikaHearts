About Lefika Hearts

Building Stronger Communities, One Heart at a Time

Our Story

Lefika Hearts NPC draws its name from the Setswana word "Lefika," meaning "rock," symbolizing strength, resilience, and a solid foundation. The organization aims to serve as a dependable support system for marginalized communities, fostering sustainable development and empowerment.

As a registered non-profit organization with the Department of Social Development, and approved as a Section 18A Public Benefit Organization by the South African Revenue Service, Lefika Hearts NPC is committed to breaking the cycles of poverty and inequality through education, skills training, humanitarian relief, and community-building initiatives.

From its operational base in Moletsane, Lefika Hearts NPC mobilizes communities toward sustainable and responsible development. The organization seeks to inspire individuals and communities to realize their full potential, creating a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, impactful, and inclusive programs that empower individuals, communities, and contribute to the socio-economic development of South Africa.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and empowered society where individuals and communities thrive through unity, hope, and sustainable development.

Our Core Values

Strength

Acting as a rock-solid foundation for those in need, providing stability, support, and hope in times of hardship.

Compassion

Serving communities with love and empathy, ensuring dignity, care, and understanding guide every action we take.

Integrity

Upholding transparency and accountability in all our initiatives, ensuring trust with donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals to build self-sufficient and resilient futures through education, skills development, and opportunity.

Diversity

Embracing and celebrating the rich cultural tapestry of South Africa, fostering inclusion, respect, and unity across all communities.

Sustainability

Promoting responsible development that benefits both present and future generations through long-term, impactful solutions.
