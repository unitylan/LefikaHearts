About Lefika Hearts

Lefika Hearts is a registered non-profit organisation dedicated to building stronger, more resilient communities through compassion, empowerment, and sustainable development.

Learn More About Us →

Founded with the belief that meaningful change starts at the heart of every community, Lefika Hearts works hand in hand with local leaders, volunteers, and partners to address real needs.

Our programmes focus on education, food security, youth development, and community upliftment — creating opportunities that empower individuals and restore dignity.

Through transparency, collaboration, and compassion, we strive to create lasting impact that goes beyond short-term relief.
