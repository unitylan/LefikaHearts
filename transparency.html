Transparency & Compliance

Our commitment to accountability and ethical practices

Our Registration & Certifications

NPO Registration

Lefika Hearts is a registered Non-Profit Organisation (NPO) in South Africa. Our NPO registration number demonstrates our compliance with the Department of Social Development's requirements for non-profit organizations.

NPO Number: 319-624

PBO Registration

As a Public Benefit Organisation (PBO), we operate under the oversight of the South African Revenue Service (SARS), ensuring we meet the highest standards of public benefit activities.

PBO Number: 930086447

Section 18A Certification

We are authorized to issue Section 18A tax certificates for qualifying donations. This means your donations are tax-deductible, providing an additional incentive to support our cause.

Section 18A Status: Active

Compliance & Governance

We adhere to all applicable laws and regulations, maintaining proper governance structures, financial controls, and reporting mechanisms to ensure accountability and transparency.

Accountability Measures

Governance Board

Our organization is governed by a dedicated board of directors who provide oversight, strategic guidance, and ensure compliance with all legal and ethical requirements. Board members serve on a voluntary basis and are committed to our mission.

Donor Reporting

We provide regular updates to donors about how their contributions are being used, including impact reports and program updates. Donors receive Section 18A certificates for qualifying donations, ensuring transparency in all financial transactions.

Code of Ethics

All staff, volunteers, and board members adhere to a strict code of ethics that emphasizes integrity, honesty, transparency, and the responsible use of resources. We maintain zero tolerance for any form of corruption or misuse of funds.
